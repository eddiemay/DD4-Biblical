Welcome to dabar.cloud

About

Dabar דבר is the word for "word", "speak" or "spoke" in Hebrew. It is what is spoken to humble servants of יהוה (). We are commanded to live by the spoken word of יהוה (). Here at dabar.cloud we present the word of יהוה alone with tools to help better understand the word so that we can guard the words of the Torah and His Commandments which is the whole duty of man ()

Resources

Calendar

An implemenation of the 364 day calendar that is described in the Book of Enoch and that we are Commanded to follow in . Complete with the Holy Days clearly marked and an entire array of events that occured throughout the history that is detailed in our Set Apart Books.

Read the Word

Ability to read and search through all our books. With over 128 and growing different books and scrolls that make up the Canon, Deuterocanon (second canon), Apocrypha and scrolls such as from the DeadSea. Gives access for all to learn and grow. The entire collection is indexed making it searchable. Go ahead and try it, see what the New Testament really says about Commandments and Law.

The Law

Read and search through the Law. An extraction of the Law from the books of Genesis through Deuteronomy with a few from Jubilees with a brief description of each Law. Read the Laws and quickly search them to understand violations.

Lessons

A collection of lessons designed to help people learn and grow. A mix of basic lessons for beginners and more advanced lessons. All can learn something from all lessons, every lesson created from scripture with lots of scriptural references.

Timeline

A timeline derived from scripture that counts from the first day of creation, it uses the Bible accounts of things like age of father upon son, years of life of individuals, year lapse of certain events and reigns of Kings. Information is pulled from all books and vetted by the Canon scriptures. A lot of work is done to make sure dates of events are the most accurate.

Games

A flashcard game that teaches the Hebrew letters in Modern, Ancient and Paleo Hebrew as well as how the letter maps to English, Arabic and Samartian letters. Quiz yourself with the letters and put yourself on the high score lists of easy, medium and hard.

AI Chat

Chat with an AI LLM (Artificial Intelligence Large Language Model) trained on the Bible. Ask it questions like: "How many sons did Abraham have?", "Where can I find the ten commandments?", and "What does the Bible say about eating pork?". Quickly find scriptures that answer life's questions.
